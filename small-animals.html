Small-Animal Owners

Housing, daily care, and enrichment tips for guinea pigs, hamsters, gerbils, and other small companions.

Housing & Habitat

Sometimes we want a pet in our life but think it’s impossible because we are not home enough or do not have enough room. Small animals such as hermit crabs, mice, gerbils, hamsters, guinea pigs, or chinchillas can be a great option. Depending on the type of animal, you can provide a comfortable environment using cages or glass aquariums of various sizes.

Your pet will need appropriate bedding or substrate, hiding spots, and enrichment items to feel safe and engaged in its environment.

Daily Care & Enrichment

Small animals need regular feeding, clean water, and gentle handling. Many species also benefit from exercise wheels, tunnels, and chew toys. It is important to learn whether your pet prefers to live alone or with a companion so you can provide the right social environment.
